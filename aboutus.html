Master Play Equipments




“Master Equipments” is renowned Playground Equipments manufacturing company based in Mumbai since 1990. ”Master Equipments “ is
an ISO 9001 : 2008 certified company & also “MSME” Goverment registered company.

From last 27 years company have earned not only the name but also faith of our prestigious client all over india through our goal of
manufacturing best quality equipments at most reasonable prices.

We manufacture all types of Outdoor Playground Equipments, like different types of swings, FRP slides, see saws, merry go rounds,
climbers, kids range, exercise range, benches, FRP dust bins, rock climbing walls, rubber flooring, multi activity play units & also outdoor
gym. equipments with modern designs.

From last 27 years we have manufactured, supplied & installed thousands of equipments not only in Mumbai & Maharashtra but also in
Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Goa, Gujarat, MP, Orissa, Rajasthan, Saurashtra etc.

We always try to give users friendly, innovative designs from time to time in Playground equipments. We always took responsibility of
giving best quality equipments, durable equipments, first priority to children safety, attractive designs & attractive colour combinations
& we have achieved our goals through our best technical & skilled company staff.

We always tried to avoid maximum use of Plastic moulded items because of there short term life & recurring expenses of our clients.
